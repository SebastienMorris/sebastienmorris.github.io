Le Dernier Courrier

Exploration/Adventure mini open world game

A narrative exploration game inspired by "La Vie de Joseph Roulin" by Pirre Michon, Vincent Van Gogh and games like The Legends of Zelda: Breath of the Wild. You play as Joseph Roulin, a postman on his final day of work. You have the freedom to explore the world, deliver letters, speak to different people and interact with different elements.

Awarded "Excellence in Art" (Busan Indie Connect Festival 2025)

What I worked on

Gameplay:

  • Bike controller and physics
  • World interactions
  • Cinematics integration

Tools:

  • Timeline based world lighting and material tool
  • World bound creation tool

Unreal Engine 5
