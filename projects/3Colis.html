Simulateur Pédagogique Colis Suspect

Suspicious package educational simulator

Educational simulator aiming to teach the students of "La Marine Nationale" the procedures for the identification of suspicious packages on a navy vessel. The simulator has two different game modes, one in first person inside the vessel and the other a 3D/2D blueprint view from outside the vessel. The students actions are saved and shown in a review summary at the end.

What I worked on

Blueprint view mode Gameplay:

  • Player controller: Flying camera controller. Handling of 2D/3D views. HUD and UI interactions
  • Game loop: Objectives system. Scriptable Objects data and in game screenshot saving for review summary. Handling of transition between first person and blueprint mode.
  • Simulation of air pressure flow of explosives withing a boat and direction towards exterior hatches.
  • wind VFX/Shader
